Scholarship
Rules of College: Identity Card - It is compulsory to bring Identity Card every day.

Attendance:
The students must be regular in college. In case the student does not fulfill the university criteria of minimum 80% attendance in classroom teaching and 90% attendance in Internship, the college will not allow her to appear in the University Examination.

Examination:
Students of all streams have to appear in every midterm/periodical examinations Disciplinary action will be taken against those who show less interest in the examination conducted from time to time.

Uniform:
The overall appearance of every student must be cultured and ethical. There will be a dress code for B.Ed. and D.El.Ed. students and it has to be strictly followed.

Discipline:
Disciplinary action will be taken if students fail to abide by the rules and regulations of the college. Misbehavior in the classroom and college premises, neglect of academic activities, use of mobile phones in the classroom, and damage to the prestige and property of the college which may be termed as an act of indiscipline and may be considered for disciplinary action by the authorities.

Documents at Admission Time:
Please Submit the following Documents with admission forms at the admission time

Documents at Admission Time: (B.Ed.)
Original 10th Mark Sheet with 2 Photocopies
Original UG/PG Marksheet with 2 Photocopies
Migration Certificate (If a Student is from a university other than Rajasthan University)
College Allotment Letter
Caste Certificate
Domicile certificate
Certificate of Special Category (Widow/ Divorce/ Defense/ Ph)
Three Passport Size Recent Photograph
Original Receipt of Fees
